What are Dinosaurs?

Many, many years before any of us were born Dinosaurs existed. Dinosaurs lived more than 150 million years ago, dinosaurs are big and scary creatures that were similar to lizards. Some dinosaurs ate plants and other meat, they were of all shapes and sizes and they lived all over the world. But, how do we know this if they lived so long ago? Scientists found their fossils to learn about them, their lifestyle and how they looked.
