What is the Solar System?

Do you ever wonder if there is anything beyond the sky? Or maybe why there's day and night, or even how the moon moves? Well it's all because of our Solar System! Our Solar System is a group of space objects that are all connected by the Sun's gravity. The Sun is a star and it is at the center of our Solar System, then there are 8 planets that rotate around the Sun. These planets are: Mercury, Venus, Earth (our home!), Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Some planets, like ours, have moons that rotate or orbit around the planet.
